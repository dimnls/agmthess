The conference of the Erasmus Generation

Welcome to the Annual General Meeting of the Erasmus Student Network, Europe's largest student conference focusing on the theme of mobility.

On 18-21 April 2019, ESN Greece will host in the city of Thessaloniki a delegation of 1000+ international students from 500+ Higher Education Institutions from 40+ countries. During 4 days, they will discuss and decide on the future of the Network and the broader role of internationally oriented youth in Europe.

This, is AGM Thessaloniki.

ESN

The Erasmus Student Network is the largest non-profit student organization in Europe. We are present in 500+ Higher Education Institutions in 40+ countries, daily assisting 220,000 international students thanks to our 15,000 volunteers.





AGM Thessaloniki

ESN Greece was selected to organize the next AGM in the city of Thessaloniki, on 18-21 April 2019. During those 4 days, 1000+ participants will take part in discussions, presentations, and votings, as well as the Flag Parade, the Mobility Fair, the International Dinner and other cultural activities.

Mobility Fair

A unique universities fair directed towards international and local students. An opportunity to meet universities and partners, and to spread the spirit of a united Europe.

Flag Parade

A city-wide celebration of mobility and interculturalism, with hundreds of students of different nationalities parading in a colorful embodiment of the European identity.
